02 — Critical Issues

Issues Requiring Immediate Action

These 5 critical issues represent broken functionality, missing accessibility attributes, or structural failures that directly harm SEO rankings, crawl budget, and user experience. Each unresolved critical issue deducts 3 points from the health score, for a total of −15 points.

CRITICAL

5 Critical Issues Found

−15 points from score
Issue Priority URLs % Total Description How to Fix
Broken Product Page (404)
fangoral.com/products/green-tea-mouthwash returns HTTP 404 with 62 inlinks pointing to it.
Critical 1 0.2% 62 internal links pointing to a dead URL — significant crawl budget waste and link equity leak. The page may have been deleted or renamed without a redirect. Add a 301 redirect from /products/green-tea-mouthwash to the nearest live product, or restore the page. Update all 62 inlinks to point to the live URL.
Images Missing Alt Text
95 images have empty or absent alt text values across product pages, banners, and collections.
Critical 95 — Blank alt text fails WCAG accessibility standards and prevents Google Image Search from indexing product visuals — a key traffic channel for e-commerce. Add descriptive alt text to all 95 images in the Shopify theme liquid files. Prioritise product images on high-traffic pages (Banner_image2, Fang_360x.png — 62 inlinks).
Images Missing Alt Attribute
93 images have no alt attribute at all in the HTML — including star.png used on 1,624 pages.
Critical 93 — Missing the alt attribute entirely (vs. empty alt text) is a more severe HTML error. star.png (review stars) appears on 1,624 pages without any alt attribute — highest-impact single fix on the site. Add alt="" or descriptive alt text to every <img> tag in theme files. Decorative images (stars, icons) should have alt="". Product images need descriptive text.
Pages Missing H1 Tag
11 pages have no H1, including the homepage (fangoral.com/) and the blog index.
Critical 11 1.9% H1 is the primary on-page keyword signal for Google. The homepage, our-story, shipping, privacy, blog, affiliate, get-hired, terms, search, cart, and CA privacy pages all lack H1s. Add a unique, keyword-targeted H1 to each page. Homepage H1 should lead with the brand's primary value proposition (e.g., "Advanced Oral Care Products for a Healthier Smile").
Pages Missing Meta Description
7 pages have no meta description, including our-story, collections/all, blog index, and affiliate page.
Critical 7 1.2% Missing meta descriptions allow Google to auto-generate snippets from page content, often producing low-CTR results. Brand and collection pages especially need crafted descriptions. Write 150–160 character meta descriptions for all 7 pages in Shopify admin (Online Store → Pages/Collections). Prioritise /pages/our-story and /collections/all first.

Warning Type URLs Impact Description Recommended Action
Canonicalised Product URLs 25 High 25 product URLs accessed via /collections/ paths are canonicalised to their /products/ equivalents. This is a Shopify default but signals that internal links are pointing to non-canonical paths, diluting link equity. Audit internal navigation, product cards, and collection pages to ensure all links use the canonical /products/ path. Update any hardcoded links in theme files. Focus on /collections/all-products/ and /collections/all/ which generate most duplicates.
Redirect Chains (3xx) 15 High 15 URLs redirect — 13 internal 301s from /collections/all/products/ to /products/ paths, plus 2 external (YouTube 303, hCaptcha 302). Internal redirect chains dilute crawl budget and slow indexing. Update all internal links pointing to /collections/all/products/ to use the final /products/ URL directly. External hCaptcha and YouTube redirects are expected and can be ignored.
Page Titles Over 60 Characters 8 Medium 8 pages have titles between 61–86 characters (longest: "Top 6 Foods & Drinks That Cause Teeth Staining" blog post at 86 chars). Titles over ~60 chars are truncated in Google SERPs with an ellipsis, hurting CTR. Shorten all titles to 50–60 characters. Remove the " – Fang Oral Care" suffix where it causes overflow — brand name appears in the favicon and URL bar anyway. Prioritise product pages (teeth-whitening-pen at 81 chars).

04 — Opportunities

Growth Quick Wins

These 12 opportunities do not directly affect the health score but represent high-value SEO improvements specific to oral care e-commerce. Implementing them can significantly expand organic visibility, improve click-through rates, and unlock rich results in Google SERPs.

OPPORTUNITIES

Medium Priority — E-Commerce & Content SEO

7 items
Opportunity Effort Impact Description Action
Product Schema (JSON-LD) Medium High No Product schema detected on product pages. Adding it enables price, availability, and review stars in Google SERPs — proven to improve CTR for oral care products. Add Product + AggregateRating JSON-LD to all Shopify product pages via theme.liquid or a dedicated schema app.
FAQ Schema on Product Pages Medium High Product pages (whitening strips, toothbrushes) typically have FAQ-style content (usage, ingredients, safety). Marking it up with FAQ schema unlocks accordion rich results in SERPs. Add FAQPage schema JSON-LD to product pages containing Q&A sections.
Breadcrumb Schema Low Medium Shopify generates breadcrumb-style URLs but no BreadcrumbList schema is present. Adding it shows the category path in SERPs (Home › Teeth Whitening › Product). Add BreadcrumbList JSON-LD schema to all product and collection pages via theme.liquid.
Open Graph & Twitter Card Meta Tags Low Medium Social sharing meta tags were not detected consistently. When products are shared on WhatsApp or Instagram, no preview card appears — reducing social referral clicks. Ensure og:title, og:description, og:image, and twitter:card tags are set on all product, collection, and blog pages in Shopify.
Consolidate Duplicate Collections Medium Medium /collections/all and /collections/all-products both exist, each generating canonical duplicates for every product. This doubles the canonical noise and dilutes crawl efficiency. Delete or redirect /collections/all-products to /collections/all (or vice versa). Update all internal links to use only one canonical collection path.
Blog-to-Product Internal Linking Low High Blog posts (e.g. "Ways to Whiten Teeth Naturally") do not link to relevant product pages (whitening strips, pen). This is a missed opportunity to pass authority and drive conversions. Add 2–3 contextual product links in each blog post. "Proven Ways to Whiten Teeth" should link to /products/fang-pro-teeth-whitening-strips and /products/teeth-whitening-pen.
Optimise Product Image Filenames Low Medium Most images have generic filenames (Banner_image2_720x.jpg, TP_20_720x.jpg). Descriptive filenames help Google Image Search index and rank product visuals. Re-upload key product images with descriptive filenames: e.g. fang-electric-toothbrush-s1-white.jpg, teeth-whitening-strips-v34.jpg before re-adding alt text.

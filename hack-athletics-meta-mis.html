04 — Strategic Insights

ROAS-led recommendations

Six data-backed actions to maximise revenue per rupee spent in the next 30 days.

01 — Star Creative
Vishav Video – Copy is your best ad
57.04× ROAS with ₹74,059 revenue on just ₹1,298 spend. This is your highest-returning creative. Budget is severely under-allocated here — this ad should be getting 3–4× more spend immediately.
→ Scale budget to ₹400–₹500/day
02 — Scale
Remarketing is your revenue engine
₹2,04,249 revenue at 47.94× ROAS — this single ad drives 32% of total account revenue. It targets warm audiences (ATC) which explains the superior conversion. Priority #1 for budget increases.
→ Increase to ₹300–₹400/day
03 — Fix
Shaker Bottle ROAS is too low
15.94× ROAS with the highest CPP (₹235) in the account. It spends ₹3,760 but generates the least revenue per rupee. The ₹3,760 budget freed from this ad, reallocated to Vishav Video – Copy, could generate ~₹1.5L extra revenue.
→ Pause or halve budget, refresh creative
04 — Investigate
Gym Bag AOV is dragging ROAS
20.28× ROAS despite 24 purchases — the ₹3,072 AOV is the second lowest in the account. Gym bags are a lower-price product vs lever belts. Consider bundling with accessories or testing upsell landing pages to raise AOV.
→ Test bundle offer / upsell page
05 — Revive
Pickle Ball Paddle is under-invested
29.09× ROAS with the lowest CPP (₹68.6) in the account — barely spent ₹411. This ad is not delivering right now but the unit economics are strong. Re-activate with ₹200/day minimum to generate meaningful data.
→ Re-activate + set ₹200/day
06 — AOV Opportunity
Vishav Video – Copy AOV = ₹6,172
The highest AOV in the account by far (₹6,172 vs blended ₹3,754) — customers coming through this creative are buying premium lever belts. This audience likely has higher purchase intent. Worth testing against Remarketing audiences too.
→ Test as Remarketing creative
Overall assessment

A blended ROAS of 32.11× on Meta is very strong — industry benchmark for e-commerce is typically 3–5×. The account is performing well overall. The opportunity is in budget reallocation: moving spend from Shaker Bottle (15.94×) to Vishav Video – Copy (57.04×) and Remarketing (47.94×) could push blended ROAS above 40× without increasing total spend.

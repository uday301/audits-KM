05 — Strategic Insights

ROAS-led recommendations

Six data-backed actions to maximise revenue per rupee spent in the next 30 days.

01 — Star Ad
Remarketing is the revenue engine
106.66× ROAS and ₹4,27,161 revenue, which is 42% of total account revenue, on just ₹4,005 spend. It also posts the lowest CPP (₹46.57) and highest AOV (₹4,967), with all three Meta rankings above average. This ad is severely under-funded for the return it delivers.
→ Increase budget to ₹300–₹400/day
02 — Scale
10mm Carbon Belt is the volume driver
74.60× ROAS on ₹2,68,018 revenue, which is 26% of the account. The strongest cold-prospecting unit, with above-average quality and engagement rankings. Headroom to push spend while ROAS holds well above the account average.
→ Raise budget 25–30%, watch ROAS floor
03 — Fix
Shaker Bottle ROAS is too low
14.15× ROAS, the only ad under 20×, with the highest CPP (₹236.29), highest CPM (₹126.6), and lowest AOV (₹3,344). It spends ₹4,017 for the weakest return in the account and is unranked. Reallocating this budget to Remarketing could generate several lakh in additional revenue.
→ Pause or halve budget, refresh creative
04 — Opportunity
Video Variant is underfunded
50.90× ROAS on only ₹441 of spend, with the best link CTR in the account (2.51%) and an above-average conversion ranking. It has not had enough budget to prove itself at scale. A controlled scale test could surface the next winner.
→ Set ₹200/day, review after 7 days
05 — Consolidate
Keep the stronger Vishav cut
Vishav Video – Copy (40.73× ROAS, ₹64,425) outperforms the original Vishav Video (29.29× ROAS, ₹26,219) on the same audience and creative concept. Running both splits learning and spend. Fund the Copy variant and retire the weaker original.
→ Pause original, back the Copy variant
06 — Refresh
Gym Bag has strong ROAS but is fatigued
39.60× ROAS and ₹1,55,182 revenue historically, yet it is currently not delivering. Link CTR is only 0.55% and the conversion-rate ranking sits in the bottom 35%, classic creative fatigue on a high-reach audience (50,789 reached). The economics justify a relaunch once the creative is refreshed.
→ New creative, then relaunch at ₹150–₹200/day
Overall assessment

A blended ROAS of 55.30× on Meta is exceptional, well above the 3–5× e-commerce benchmark, with ₹10,20,299 in revenue on ₹18,452 of spend. Revenue is concentrated in Remarketing (42%) and 10mm Carbon Belt (26%), which together drive 68% of the account. The clearest win is reallocating Shaker Bottle's ₹4,017 (14.15×) toward Remarketing (106.66×) and the Video Variant (50.90×), which would lift blended ROAS further without increasing total spend.

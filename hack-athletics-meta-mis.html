04 — Strategic Insights

Actions & recommendations

Six data-backed recommendations to reduce blended CPP, reallocate budget, and improve overall account efficiency in the next 30 days.

01 — Scale
Double down on Remarketing
At ₹80.3 CPP with 58 purchases, Remarketing is 32% cheaper than the blended average. Increasing budget here directly lowers overall account CPP. Switch to campaign budget and raise by ₹100–₹200/day.
→ Increase daily budget by ₹150
02 — Revive
Restart Pickle Ball Paddle
Paused with only ₹813 spent but a CPP of ₹81.3 — nearly as efficient as Remarketing with very low frequency (1.62×). The audience is barely touched. Allocate ₹200/day and run for 2 more weeks before judging.
→ Resume + set ₹200/day budget
03 — Pause
Pause or restructure Shaker Bottle
₹3,605 spent at ₹240.4 CPP — the worst performer by a wide margin. With only 15 purchases and small reach (15K), this campaign is burning budget inefficiently. Pause it and test a new creative angle.
→ Pause now, refresh creative
04 — Optimise
Gym Bag — narrow the audience
Highest impressions (87K) and reach (47K) but only 22 conversions at ₹160 CPP. The broad reach suggests targeting is too wide. Add purchase intent or lookalike signals, or narrow to fitness enthusiasts.
→ Tighten targeting or add LAL
05 — Scale
Influencer Video has room to grow
CPP ₹108.7 with the lowest frequency (1.97×) of all active campaigns. The audience is not fatigued. This ad format has headroom — increase spend by ₹100/day and monitor CPP over the next 7 days.
→ Increase to ₹300/day
06 — Rebalance
Reallocate Shaker Bottle budget
Freeing ₹200/day from Shaker Bottle creates an immediate reallocation opportunity. Split it: ₹100/day to Remarketing (best CPP) + ₹100/day to Pickle Ball revival. At current CPPs, this could yield +3–4 incremental purchases/day.
→ Reallocate ₹100+₹100/day
Projected impact

Implementing recommendations 01, 02, 03, and 06 without increasing total daily spend should lower the blended CPP from ₹117.5 to approximately ₹90–₹95 — a ~20% improvement — within 2 weeks.

Watch list — Lever Belt frequency

Both Lever Belt campaigns (10mm Carbon Belt and Influencer Video) are targeting overlapping audiences. The 10mm campaign's frequency of 2.82× is the highest in the account. Consider merging ad sets or adding audience exclusions to prevent overlap and fatigue.

02 — Our Audit Services

Five Audits. One Complete Picture.

Each audit is a standalone deep-dive — or combine them for a full-stack digital health check. Every audit is hand-crafted by our specialists, not auto-generated by a tool.

SEO Audit

A forensic look at your website's search performance — from crawlability to content quality to authority signals. We map exactly why you're not ranking and what it will take to get there.

Social Media Audit

A channel-by-channel review of your social media presence — content strategy, engagement patterns, profile optimisation, and competitive positioning across Instagram, LinkedIn, and more.

Content Audit

We inventory and evaluate all your existing content — blogs, landing pages, videos — to identify what's performing, what needs updating, what should be retired, and what gaps exist.

Website Performance & UX Audit

Speed, usability, and conversion rate optimisation — we assess your website's technical performance and user experience to identify friction points costing you conversions, even if your traffic is healthy.

03 — Our Process

How a Kerkar Media Audit Works

From brief to delivery, here's exactly what happens when you commission an audit from us.

Step 01 — Day 1
Discovery & Access

We send you a simple onboarding form to collect access credentials — Google Analytics, Search Console, Ads account, social profiles. No unnecessary back-and-forth. You fill it once, we handle the rest.

Step 02 — Days 2–5
Deep-Dive Analysis

Our specialists go through every data point — crawl logs, ad account history, content performance, social analytics. We benchmark against your competitors and industry standards. Everything is manually reviewed, not auto-generated.

Step 03 — Days 5–7
Report & Roadmap Creation

Findings are compiled into a structured audit report with a prioritised action roadmap. Every issue is assigned a severity level (Critical / High / Medium) and paired with a specific, actionable recommendation.

Step 04 — Day 7–8
Walkthrough Call

We walk you through the full report on a 30–45 minute video call. You leave the call knowing exactly what to fix, in what order, and why — with a recording shared for your team's reference.

06 — FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know before commissioning an audit.

What is a digital marketing audit?
A digital marketing audit is a comprehensive review of your brand's online presence — covering SEO, paid ads, social media, content, and website performance — to identify gaps, errors, and opportunities. The output is a prioritised roadmap that tells you exactly what to fix, in what order, and why.
How long does an audit take?
Most standalone audits are delivered within 5–7 business days. A full-stack audit covering all five channels typically takes 7–10 business days. We'll confirm the exact timeline at the time of briefing.
Do I need to give you access to my accounts?
Yes — read-only access to relevant platforms (Google Analytics, Search Console, Ads account, social profiles) is required for a thorough audit. We use a secure onboarding form and never store your credentials beyond the audit period.
What do I receive at the end?
You receive a detailed audit report (PDF + HTML), a prioritised action roadmap, relevant data sheets (keyword gaps, competitor benchmarks, ad scorecards), a 30-day quick-win checklist, and a 30–45 minute walkthrough call with a recording.
Will you implement the changes too, or just audit?
Audits are a standalone service — we give you the full picture and roadmap. However, many clients choose to engage us for implementation after the audit. We offer SEO, paid media, social media, and content services scoped based on audit findings.
Do you work with businesses outside India?
Yes. While Kerkar Media is based in India, we work with brands globally. Our audits are platform-agnostic and applicable to any market or geography.
How is a Kerkar Media audit different from a free tool report?
Free tools generate data dumps. Our audits are manually interpreted by specialists who understand your industry and business context. We don't just surface issues — we explain why they matter, what to prioritise, and exactly how to fix them.

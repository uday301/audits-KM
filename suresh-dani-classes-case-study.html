Capturing the searches students actually make

Challenge

SDC’s organic traffic leaned heavily on brand searches. The decision-stage journeys Mumbai students run — choosing an FYJC college, comparing streams, calculating an MHT-CET percentile — weren’t bringing them to the site.

Solution

Kerkar Media built a cluster of high-intent guide content mapped to the student decision journey — FYJC science & commerce college guides, an MHT-CET percentile explainer, government medical college lists and stream-specific college pages — with on-page and technical optimisation behind it.

Results

Non-brand clicks rose +168% (663 → 1,774). Six new content pages went from near zero to a combined ≈3,900 clicks a month, opening an audience the brand-only footprint never reached.

Climbing into the positions that earn the click

Challenge

The site ranked for strong informational queries but sat outside the first page on desktop — an average position near 21 — so the impressions rarely converted into visits.

Solution

We prioritised pages by intent-to-effort ratio and improved titles, on-page structure, internal linking and content depth to move them from page two into striking distance on page one.

Results

Desktop average position improved from 20.9 to 8.7 and mobile from 6.6 to 5.2. Impressions grew +127% (753K → 1.71M) and total clicks +128% (4,660 → 10,620).
